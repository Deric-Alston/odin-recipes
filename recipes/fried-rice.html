Fried Rice Recipe

Recipe

An easy and delicious fried rice dish!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups cooked rice
  • 2 Tbs sesame oil
  • 1 small white onion chopped
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots thawed
  • 2-3 Tbs soy sauce
  • 2 eggs lightly beaten
  • 2 Tbsp chopped green onions optional

Steps

  1. Preheat a large skillet to medium heat
  2. Pour sesame oil in the skillet and add white onions, peas and carrots until tender
  3. Slide the onions, peas, and carrots to the side and pour the beaten eggs onto the other side.
  4. Using a spatula, scramble the eggs and once cooked, mix the eggs with the vegetable mix
  5. Add the rice to the veggie and egg mixture
  6. Pour the soy sauce on top
  7. Stir and try the rice and veggie mix until heated through and combined.
  8. Add chopped green onions if desired
