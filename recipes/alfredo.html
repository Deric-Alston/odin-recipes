Chicken Alfredo Penne Recipe

Recipe

This easy chicken alfredo penne recipe is a Italian staple packed with tons of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 chicken breast
  • 2 TBS butter
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 16 oz penne pasta
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Sauce

  • 2 TBS butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 TBS flour
  • 2 Cups milk
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. In a pan melt butter then add the chicken breast
  2. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Cook 8-10 minutes or until fully cooked, then set aside
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and add the garlic. Cook until the garlic begins to soften
  4. Add half of the flour to the garlic and butter, stirring until incorporated and sauce begins to thicken
  5. Pour in the milk and stir until completely and sauce begins to thicken
  6. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil, and stir well
  7. Add parmesan cheese and sitr until melted
  8. Pour the sauce over cooked penne pasta, add the chicken and mix well
  9. Add parsley and extra parmesan, mix well
